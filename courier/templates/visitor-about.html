about us

GTA Quick Courier started in 1981. It originally served all of the GTAA.

Growing at a steady pace, GTA Quick Courier acquired the contract to deliver to all of southern Ontario and Quebec branches of Central Guaranty Trust. GTA Quick Courier was its exclusive courier for over ten years

After the closure of Central Guaranty Trust in 1992, many of its large subsidiaries such as Wells Fargo, CGU (Canadian Insurance) and Aviva Canada remained with us. GTA Quick Courier also went on to offer a wide range of services such as storage, moving, distribution and mailroom services.

We are also associated with major couriers to ship all over Canada, United States and International. The large volume of business allow our clients substantial savings

GTA Quick Courier is constantly expanding in order to accommodate and to meet the multiple needs of ever-changing business world.

  • Our Reliable and Steady Drivers are Professional, Courteous and very Knowledgeable.
  • We provide scheduled Post Office Drop Offs/Pick Ups, Bank Deposits and Inter-Office Mail Runs.
  • Gta Quick Courier has proudly maintained and served the same clients for over 35 years.
